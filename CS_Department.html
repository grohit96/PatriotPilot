About Us

The Department of Computer Science at Mason, the largest and fastest-growing CS department in Virginia, is a cornerstone of the College of Engineering and Computing. Hosting nearly 80 full-time faculty and over 3,200 students across its undergraduate and graduate programs, the department offers a diverse array of studies including two Bachelor of Science degrees, four Masters degrees in specialized areas, and Ph.D. programs in Computer Science and Information Technology. Emphasizing a "Computing for All" philosophy, our curriculum bridges computing with various academic fields, preparing students for interdisciplinary applications in a technology-driven world.

Our faculty are leaders in their fields, engaging in groundbreaking research and innovative teaching, supported by an array of national awards such as NSF CAREER and AFOSR Young Investigator awards. The department's commitment extends beyond academia into industry and community engagement through an active External Advisory Board, which includes Mason alumni and industry experts who ensure our programs align with the latest technological advances. Upholding GMUs mission, we strive for inclusivity and diversity, aiming to increase participation from underrepresented groups in computing, reflecting the dynamic and diverse nature of our university.
